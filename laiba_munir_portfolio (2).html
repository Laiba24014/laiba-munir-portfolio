Research Project

Deepfake & AI-Generated Image Detection using VLM

A research-driven Vision-Language Model system that detects deepfake videos and AI-generated images produced by tools like ChatGPT (DALL-E), Gemini, and Stable Diffusion. The project involved custom dataset collection of real vs. AI-generated images, followed by fine-tuning a VLM to reason about visual artifacts, facial inconsistencies, and lighting anomalies beyond traditional pixel-level forensics.

Key Features
  • Custom dataset: real images + AI-generated images from ChatGPT, Gemini & Stable Diffusion
  • Fine-tuned VLM for explainable fake-vs-real reasoning
  • Detects both deepfake videos and fully AI-generated images
  • Facial artifact, lighting & texture inconsistency analysis
  • Confidence-scored verdict with visual evidence highlighting
